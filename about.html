* ABOUT US! *

We are so happy you’re here! Grab a cookie, take a seat, and let us introduce myself.

Rachel

Hi, thanks so much for stopping by. I’m Rachel and I operate this blog. I bake too much, watch way more hours of Food Network programing than I care to admit publicly, I stay up too late and eat way too much sugar. By day, I teach middle school, but during the evenings and weekends I am in the kitchen cooking and baking delectable dishes and desserts. My dream is to have my own cooking channel and I hope that this blog help me reach my goal!

Sara

I’m Sara. I like to take pictures, write, and bake. Scratch that, I’m addicted to baking. The kitchen is where my creative juices flow and We Love Food! is where I can share it all with you. Often with a lot of sprinkles and even more exclamation points. I’m a self-taught, mom-taught, grandma-taught baker. My favorite dessert is apple pie, coffee keeps me sane, running keeps me content, yoga keeps me balanced. The motivation for this site to me during my mid-twenties. At the time I worked in finance and felt a little lost in both my career and my life. I tapped into my many, many, MANY memories being in the kitchen when I was younger, picked up a camera (taught myself how to use it!), and the rest is history.

* About We Love Food! *

We started this blog to share the recipes we baked and cooked on the weekends and holidays. Our goal is to help readers feel like they are a master in the kitchen! And, more importantly, we want everyone to enjoy the experience of making really good food. We hope you find our blog inviting, fun and full of deliciously addictive recipes and photography!

In cooking and baking, we unite!
